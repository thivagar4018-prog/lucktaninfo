Our courses are structured for all levels. The Beginner Track requires zero prior coding experience. The Developer and Pro Tracks cater to software engineers and Web2 developers who want to build and deploy smart contracts, DeFi protocols, and dApps professionally.

Is there a private community?

Yes, all students join our 10,000+ member Discord community. Developer and Pro track students get access to VIP channels where senior blockchain engineers share code reviews, live sessions, hackathon opportunities, and cutting-edge protocol updates.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a no-questions-asked 14-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions.

Are there any discounts or bundles?

We regularly drop exclusive bundle deals and flash discounts so you can find more courses for less and level up your skills.

Yes, all materials are fully online and self-paced. You can pause, review, and learn at whatever speed is comfortable for you.
